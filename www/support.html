Seven years ago, in Ramadan 1430, it all started. Young minds came together to make a mark, spreading pristine "dawah to the people". In these years, tens of terabytes of unique Nigerian dawah have been delivered across almost all countries of the world. Our dawahcast project now has over 200G of lecture audios in various Nigerian languages. The Ramadan e-Tafseer project is an interesting one where recordings of tafseer sessions from multiple locations in Nigeria are uploaded daily. We started with 7 centers in Ramadan 1433AH and the number has been growing significantly since then; now features LIVE video, LIVE audio and daily recordings.

We have set up a studio where we record & run the Dawahnigeria Quran Project, an ambitious project to adequately contribute the Nigerian 'touch' to the growing number of available Quran recitations online. Our quiz project on DeenQuiz.com with over 4000 questions with options and brief explanation is rated by some as the most comprehensive Islamic quiz online. Mobile apps have also been developed to enhance the mobile experience.

Quranopedia.com project is another huge one. It is an ambitious initiative aimed at bringing the Quran closer to people, with a vision of becoming an encyclopedia of the Quran. Kindly visit, too enormous to describe!

The list goes on and on, please visit Our Initiatives for an overview of our projects.

Sadly, we hope all these would not come to an abrupt end. What it costs to keep the projects running is considerably more than we can continue to bear. The exchange rate and economic situation generally makes us close to extinction almost each time we have to fulfil our ever-increasing obligations. (read more - Operating Needs )

What numerous people have passionately worked for, day and night, for years, is in need of urgent and consistent help.

Imagine the reward for facilitating propagation of dawah at this scale ! Kindly donate. Please contact us for comments / suggestions. And all power and glory belongs to Allaah.
